The Best Movies According to Chantel

My Top 3 movies of all time

Spirited Away

Spirited away is a Anime movies about a girl who loses her parents to the spirit world due to a curse. She must help her friend Haku find the missing pieces in order to break this curse and return back to the human world and get her parents back.

The Princes' Bride

The Princes' bride is a story about a jelous friend who is hellbound on stopping the marriage between her best friend and the Prince of Zazu due to her undying love for the prince. She goes through all obstacles to steal his attention which eventually leads her to setting a plot to harm her bestfriend which leads to her untimily demise instead.

Why did I get married 1 and 2

Why did I get married 1 and 2 is a story about 4 couples who have an annual couple's retreat to celebrate their marriages. Each couple has their own story which describes its ups and downs along with why they got married. I love this film because it really capsulates different dynamics in marriages and how to navigate them.
